Marta Garcia-Casado


I am an AIML resident at Apple MLR, in the Paris team led by Marco Cuturi.

I am also a PhD student in Computational Semantics at the Universitat Pompeu Fabra (Barcelona) supervised by Elisenda Bernal and Thomas Brochhagen.

I am interested in language models and how they can detect and track semantic change. In particular, in my research, I study the automation of verbal semantic neology detection.
